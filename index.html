🔬 Research Focus

My research explores two complementary directions: multimodal large language models (focusing on representation learning and interactive reasoning) and brain-inspired intelligence (with emphasis on spiking neural networks and efficient learning algorithms). I aim to advance efficient, generalizable, and biologically plausible AI systems through both theoretical innovation and practical deployment.

Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) ANN-SNN Conversion Brain-Inspired Multimodal Models Generative Models Quantization-Aware Training & Post-Training Quantization
